## Notes {#notes}

1.  Don't multitask
2.  Don't Pontificate
3.  Open-ended questions
4.  Go with the flow
5.  If you don't know, say you don't know
6.  Don't equate your experience with theirs
7.  Try not to repeat yourself
8.  Stay out of the weeds (too many details)
9.  Listen
10. Be brief
